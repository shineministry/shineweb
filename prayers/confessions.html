Confessions

Prayers for the Sacrament of Reconciliation

How to Go to Confession

  1. Before Confession: Examine your conscience, pray for guidance (Come Holy Spirit), and make a sincere resolution to amend your life.
  2. Enter the confessional: "Bless me, Father, for I have sinned. It has been [time] since my last confession."
  3. Confess your sins honestly and completely, including the number of times for serious sins.
  4. Listen to the priest's counsel and accept your penance.
  5. Pray the Act of Contrition with sincere repentance.
  6. Receive absolution as the priest prays the prayer of forgiveness.
  7. After Confession: Thank God for His mercy and perform your penance.

Before Confession: Preparation

Prayer for Guidance

Come, Holy Spirit, enlighten my mind and my heart. Help me to see my sins as God sees them. Give me the grace to make a good confession and to receive God's merciful forgiveness. Amen.

Examination of Conscience (Based on the Ten Commandments)

Take time to reflect honestly on each area of your life:

1. I am the Lord your God; you shall not have strange gods before Me

  • Have I put other things before God? (money, work, pleasure, relationships)
  • Have I been involved in superstition, fortune-telling, or the occult?
  • Have I doubted or denied my faith?

2. You shall not take the name of the Lord your God in vain

  • Have I used God's name irreverently?
  • Have I used vulgar or profane language?
  • Have I broken an oath or vow?

3. Remember to keep holy the Lord's Day

  • Have I missed Mass on Sundays or Holy Days of obligation?
  • Have I arrived late or left early without good reason?
  • Have I done unnecessary work on Sunday?

4. Honor your father and your mother

  • Have I been disobedient or disrespectful to my parents?
  • Have I neglected my duties to my family?
  • Have I treated my spouse, children, or siblings with love and respect?

5. You shall not kill

  • Have I harmed anyone physically or emotionally?
  • Have I entertained thoughts of hatred, anger, or revenge?
  • Have I abused alcohol, drugs, or my own body?

6. You shall not commit adultery

  • Have I engaged in sexual activity outside of marriage?
  • Have I entertained impure thoughts or looked at impure images?
  • Have I been faithful to my marriage vows?

7. You shall not steal

  • Have I taken something that does not belong to me?
  • Have I cheated or been dishonest in business?
  • Have I wasted time or resources at work?

8. You shall not bear false witness

  • Have I lied or been deceitful?
  • Have I gossiped or spread rumors?
  • Have I judged others unfairly?

9. You shall not covet your neighbor's spouse

  • Have I entertained lustful thoughts or desires?
  • Have I willfully entertained sexual fantasies?

10. You shall not covet your neighbor's goods

  • Have I been envious of others' possessions or success?
  • Have I been greedy or materialistic?
  • Have I failed to share with those in need?

During Confession: Act of Contrition

O my God, I am heartily sorry for having offended Thee, and I detest all my sins because of Thy just punishments, but most of all because they offend Thee, my God, who art all good and deserving of all my love. I firmly resolve, with the help of Thy grace, to sin no more and to avoid the near occasion of sin. Amen.

After Confession: Prayer of Thanksgiving

I thank You, Lord Jesus, for Your infinite mercy. You have washed away my sins and restored me to grace. Help me to live in Your love and to avoid sin. I offer my penance in union with Your suffering for the salvation of souls. Amen.
